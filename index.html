Your Life is About to Change

Calvin Chess is a chaotic game of chess, wherein the rules randomly change. Inspired by Calvinball from the Calvin and Hobbes comic strip. Fight aliens with batman, survive a nuclear apocalypse, and use your teleportation powers to destroy your enemy's king.
